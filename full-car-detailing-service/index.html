Frequently Asked Questions

Full detailing covers a foam wash and decontamination, paint correction/polishing, wax or sealant protection, a deep interior clean (seats, carpets, dashboard, vents), wheels and tyres, glass and chrome polishing, and cabin sanitization.

A wash cleans the surface. Detailing restores the car — correcting paint, decontaminating, deep-cleaning the interior and adding protection — for a far deeper, longer-lasting result.

Full detailing typically takes several hours depending on your car's size and condition. Your detailer will give you an accurate time estimate before starting.

Pricing depends on your car's size, paint condition and the protection you choose. Message us on WhatsApp with your car details for a quick, transparent quote.

A full detail every 4-6 months keeps your paint protected and interior fresh, with regular washes in between — more often if you park outdoors in Dehradun/NCR dust and sun.
