Beyond the Shine: Creating Professional Careers in Dehradun

When you book a Carmaa service, you aren't just getting a clean car—you are supporting a mission to professionalize the automotive service industry in Dehradun. We are proud to be a platform that empowers local youth with technical skills, financial stability, and a career they can be proud of.

From Laborers to Technicians

In the traditional car wash industry, workers are often treated as temporary labor with little to no training. At Carmaa, we call our team members **Technicians**. Every person who joins us undergoes a rigorous 15-day certification program covering chemical science, surface physics, and customer service. We transform raw talent into professional detailers.

Success Story: Vivek’s Journey

"Before Carmaa, I was working odd jobs with no fixed income. Now, I have a professional uniform, a specialized kit, and a stable monthly salary. I’ve learned about the science of paint protection and how to talk to premium car owners in Mussoorie Road. I don't just wash cars; I provide a professional service. My family is proud of what I do."

Fair Wages and Health Benefits

We believe that a happy technician provides the best service. That’s why we offer wages that are significantly higher than the industry average, along with health insurance and flexible schedules. By providing a dignified workplace, we ensure that our team members in Prem Nagar, Jakhan, and Dalanwala are committed to excellence.

Building the Future of Dehradun

As we grow, we continue to hire and train more local talent. Our goal is to create 500+ professional car detailing jobs in Uttarakhand by 2027. We are proving that with the right technology and training, doorstep services can be a powerful engine for local economic growth.
