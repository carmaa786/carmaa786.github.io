Detailing Cost in Dehradun

Understanding the Cost of Full Car Detailing Services in Dehradun

Dehradun is known for its scenic beauty, but let's be honest: the combination of city traffic, dusty mountain roads, and intense monsoons takes a serious toll on our cars. When a simple wash isn't enough, professional detailing is the answer. But how much should you actually expect to pay for a quality job in Dehradun?

Pricing can vary wildly from a roadside garage to a high-end detailing studio. Here’s a transparent breakdown of what you're paying for and what the going rates look like.

What Dictates the Price?

Not all detailing jobs are created equal. The cost is primarily driven by three factors: the size of your vehicle (a hatchback costs less than a full-size SUV), the current condition of the car (heavily oxidized paint or heavily stained interiors take hours of extra labor), and the quality of the products being used.

Average Cost Breakdown in Dehradun

1. Basic Interior & Exterior Detailing: ₹1,500 – ₹3,500
This usually covers a thorough exterior foam wash, basic wax application, interior vacuuming, dashboard polishing, and window cleaning. It's a great maintenance package to refresh the car.

2. Deep Interior Dry Cleaning & Sanitization: ₹1,000 – ₹2,500
If your seats have coffee stains or the cabin smells stale, this is what you need. It involves chemical extraction of the seats and carpets, roof cleaning, and AC vent sanitization.

3. Exterior Paint Correction (Rubbing & Polishing): ₹2,500 – ₹5,000
This is where the magic happens for dull paint. Professionals use compounds and machine polishers to remove swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation, restoring the factory shine.

4. Ceramic Coating: ₹12,000 – ₹35,000+
The ultimate protection. The high price tag comes from the labor-intensive paint correction required before application, and the high cost of the liquid ceramic polymer itself. This is an investment that lasts for years.
