Where Convenience Meets Your City: Our Growing Footprint

One of the most frequent questions we get on our social media is: "When are you coming to my city?" Today, we’re excited to give you a full tour of where Carmaa Car Care is currently operating and why we’ve chosen these hubs to revolutionize doorstep car detailing.

The Heart of Carmaa: Dehradun

Born and incubated at Graphic Era (TBI), Dehradun is our home. We started with a simple mission: to save water while providing premium care. Today, we cover almost every corner of the Doon Valley, from the bustling lanes of Rajpur Road to the residential hubs of Jakhan and Prem Nagar. If you see a sleek white van with the Carmaa logo near Centrio Mall, that’s us ready to make a car shine!

The Fast Lane: Gurgaon & Noida

In the high-paced corporate world of Gurgaon and Noida, time is the ultimate luxury. We realized that busy professionals in DLF Phase 3 or Noida Sector 62 shouldn't have to waste their precious weekends waiting at a dusty local garage. Our quick wash has become a favorite for those who value efficiency without compromising on quality.

Customer Insight: Rahul from Cyber City

"I used to spend 3 hours every Sunday just to get my SUV cleaned. Now, I book a Carmaa technician through the app while I'm having breakfast. By the time I'm ready for my morning drive, the car is sparkling. It’s not just a wash; it’s 3 hours of my life back."

Expanding Across Delhi NCR

Our footprint is rapidly expanding across the National Capital Region. We are strategically increasing our fleet size to ensure that whether you are in South Delhi or East Delhi, a professional Carmaa technician is never more than a few taps away. Our goal is to bring the same eco-friendly, waterless technology to every major metro in India by 2027.

What’s Next?

We are currently analyzing data for our next major launch. Will it be Chandigarh? Jaipur? Or perhaps Bangalore? Stay tuned to our social handles for the big announcement. If we aren't in your city yet, we promise we are working hard to get there!
