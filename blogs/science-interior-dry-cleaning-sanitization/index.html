The Science of Interior Dry Cleaning and Sanitization

Most car owners spend 2-3 hours a day inside their vehicles. Yet, the interior is often the most neglected part of car care. It’s not just about the "new car smell"; it’s about the millions of bacteria and allergens that thrive in your upholstery and AC vents. At Carmaa, we treat interior car cleaning as a science, not just a chore.

Why Vacuuming Isn’t Enough

A standard vacuum picks up loose dust, but it doesn't touch the oils, sweat, and deep-seated allergens trapped in the fibers of your seats. In a high-humidity environment like Noida or Dehradun, these organic materials become a breeding ground for mold and dust mites. Professional dry cleaning uses specialized chemicals to break these bonds without soaking the material.

Especially for families with kids, this is the most important service we offer."

The Carmaa 5-Step Sanitization Process

Our 2026 interior protocol involves more than just cleaning. We follow a strict 5-step process: De-cluttering, Compressed Air Blowout, Chemical Agitation, Extraction, and finally, UVC or Steam Sanitization. This ensures that 99.9% of germs are eliminated from high-touch surfaces like the steering wheel, gear shift, and door handles.
