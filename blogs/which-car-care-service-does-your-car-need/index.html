Which Car Care Service Does Your Car Need?

So Many Services — Which One Does Your Car Actually Need?

Open the Carmaa app and you'll see a full menu: waterless wash, interior dry clean, deep spa, polishing, ceramic and graphene coatings, steam wash, glass coating. It's genuinely everything your car could need — but if you just want a clean, healthy car, which one do you actually book? This guide cuts through it: tell us your car's problem, and we'll point you to the right service.

Quick Matcher

  • Routine clean & shine? → In & Out Car Wash
  • Stained, smelly fabric seats? → Interior Dry Clean / Spa
  • Months of neglect, deep grime? → Deep Cleaning or Full Detailing
  • Dull, swirled, scratched paint? → Polish & Wax
  • Want long-term protection? → Ceramic / Graphene Coating

Just Keeping It Clean: The Doorstep Car Wash

If your car is in regular use and you simply want to keep it dust-free and shiny, you need a routine wash — not detailing. Carmaa's in & out car wash uses a waterless, paint-safe method that cleans the body, glass, tyres and a quick interior wipe-down, right outside your home. Done every week or two, it's all most daily-driven cars need to stay looking fresh.

Dirty Seats, Stains or a Musty Smell: Interior Dry Clean

A shiny exterior with grubby, smelly seats is the most common mismatch we see. If the problem is inside — food stains, dust embedded in fabric, pet hair, a damp odour — what you want is an interior cleaning or our car dry clean. It deep-cleans seats, carpets and panels with low-moisture foam, so the cabin is sanitised and fresh without soaking your upholstery.

Months of Neglect: Deep Cleaning or Full Detailing

If it's been a long time — caked grime, dull paint, grubby cabin all at once — a single wash won't cut it. Go for a deep cleaning spa or the complete full car detailing service, which combines exterior correction, wheels, deep interior cleaning and protection in one session to bring a tired car back to near-showroom condition.

Dull or Swirled Paint: Polish & Wax

Notice fine swirl marks, water spots or a flat, lifeless finish even after washing? That's the paint's clear coat, and washing won't fix it. A polish and wax service machine-polishes out the imperfections and lays down a protective wax for real depth and gloss.

Long-Term Protection: Ceramic & Graphene Coating

If you want your car to stay glossy and easy to clean for years — not weeks — a coating is the upgrade. A ceramic coating or graphene coating bonds to the paint and makes water, dust and grime slide off, while protecting against fading. Explore the options under paint protection & coatings.

Rainy-Season Visibility & Hygiene: Glass Coating and Steam Wash

Two specialists worth knowing: a glass & surface coating makes rain bead off your windshield for safer monsoon driving, and a steam wash sanitises the cabin with high-temperature steam — ideal if anyone in the family has allergies or you want a chemical-light, hygienic clean.

Still Not Sure? We'll Help You Pick

When in doubt, tell us your car's situation and your budget and we'll recommend the right service — no upselling.
