How Carmaa Saved 3.5 Million Liters of Water

Home / Blogs / Water Savings Story

Numbers tell a story, but at Carmaa, they represent a mission. Since our journey began, we’ve successfully saved over **3.5 million liters of water**. That’s enough to fill nearly 1,400 Olympic-sized swimming pools. But why does this matter so much for a city like **Dehradun**?

Born at Graphic Era TBI: An Innovation Story

Carmaa wasn’t just "another business." It was incubated at the **Graphic Era Technology Business Incubation (TBI)** center. Our founders were obsessed with a single problem: the thousands of liters wasted every day by traditional car wash centers. By merging chemical engineering with doorstep convenience, we developed a 2026-standard waterless technology that cleans better than high-pressure jets without wasting a single drop.

Technician’s Perspective: Sabir’s Insights

"When I first started, people in **Jakhan** and **Rajpur Road** were skeptical. They thought 'waterless' meant 'scratchy.' But once they saw our specialty lubricants in action, their eyes lit up. We use only about 200ml of product for a whole car, whereas a traditional wash wastes 150 liters. My favorite part is seeing a client's surprise when their car shines brighter and stays clean longer because of the protective barrier we leave behind."

The Science of Waterless Shine

Traditional washing uses water to blast away dirt, often taking parts of the clear coat with it. Our waterless tech works through encapsulation. The spray breaks down the dirt and lifts it into a protective bubble, which we then gently wipe away with specialized microfiber towels. It’s better for your car, better for the planet, and significantly more efficient.
