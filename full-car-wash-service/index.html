What makes Carmaa's full car wash eco-friendly?

Traditional car washes consume 150 to 300 litres of fresh water per wash, causing enormous waste. Carmaa uses optimised water spray technology, biodegradable cleaning agents, and offers waterless steam wash options that consume as little as 2 litres of water. This approach aligns with future 2026 sustainable car care standards and reduces environmental impact without compromising on cleaning quality. Every wash uses 100% biodegradable foam and rinse-aid that are safe for your paint, your driveway, and the local groundwater systems.
