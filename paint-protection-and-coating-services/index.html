Wax Protection vs. Full Coating — Which Do You Need?

Wax protection (ceramic or graphene wax) is a quick, affordable treatment that adds a hydrophobic, glossy layer lasting a few months — perfect for regular upkeep and that fresh-from-the-detailer look. A full ceramic or graphene coating bonds for years and offers much stronger, longer-term protection.

Not sure which fits your car and budget? Our team will recommend honestly — many owners use wax protection between annual coatings to keep the shine topped up.
