# Carmaa SEO & Technical Optimization Summary

This document summarizes the technical SEO, content strategy, and community engagement optimizations implemented in this pull request.

## 🚀 Key Improvements

### 1. Programmatic Content & Local SEO
- **New City Landing Pages**: Optimized high-intent pages for **Noida** and **Dehradun** with city-specific keywords and comparisons.
- **Service-Specific FAQs**: Injected H3-based FAQ sections into the Homepage and About page to capture **AEO/GEO** (Answer/Generative Engine Optimization) traffic.
- **Internal Link Infrastructure**: Established a "Link & Sync" architecture connecting services to blogs and vice-versa, improving the site's semantic graph.

### 2. Technical SEO & Schema
- **JSON-LD Schema Integration**: Implemented and verified multiple schema types:
    - `LocalBusiness`: Consolidated for Dehradun/Noida.
    - `FAQPage`: Structured for service-specific answers.
    - `BlogPosting`: For all new educational content.
- **Title & Meta Standardization**: Every page now features a unique, keyword-optimized `<title>` and `<meta name="description">` focusing on *convenience*, *sustainability*, and *waterless technology*.
- **Canonical Tags**: Verified canonical URLs across all pages to prevent duplicate content issues.

### 3. Content Expansion (Blogs)

1.  **Home Car Wash Guide (Noida)**: Focused on professional convenience.
2.  **Sustainability Story (Graphic Era TBI)**: Leveraging brand authority and waterless tech milestones.
3.  **Luxury Car Monsoon Care (Dehradun)**: Highlighting Hydrophobic/Ceramic Coating benefits.
4.  **Interior Science & Sanitization**: Deep-dive into cabin health standards.

### 4. UI/UX & Performance
- **Comparison Tables**: Added "Doorstep vs. Garage" tables to primary service pages with standardized UI.
- **Mobile Responsiveness**: All new components (FAQ blocks, tables, related reading) are fully responsive and Core Web Vitals friendly.
- **Style Consolidation**: Centralized styling in `css/common.css` and `css/service.css` for site-wide consistency.

### 5. Community Engagement Strategy
- **Reddit Strategy**: Developed a value-first, non-promotional engagement strategy for `r/Noida`, `r/Dehradun`, and `r/Gurgaon` using expert technician expertise. (See `reddit_strategy_drafts.md`).
