Premium Car Spa — The Ultimate Deep Clean at Your Doorstep

What is a Premium Car Spa and How is it Different?

A premium car spa is an advanced vehicle detailing treatment. While a standard wash only removes surface dust, our premium car spa is a comprehensive, 20-step interior and exterior rejuvenation process. It targets hard-to-reach areas, dissolves deep paint contaminants, sanitises interior upholstery, and deodorizes the cabin. In Dehradun's conditions — where dust, pollen, and mud from the surrounding hills accumulate quickly — a standard wash can leave mineral deposits on your paint. A premium car spa decontaminates the exterior and restores the cabin's hygiene, keeping your car looking and feeling like new.

Additionally, our premium car spa uses professional tools like clay bars, machine polishers, and steam cleaners. We treat all high-touch surfaces, including door handles, steering wheels, and gear shifters, with antibacterial agents. This thorough cleaning ensures a hygienic and fresh environment inside your car.

The 20-Step Carmaa Premium Car Spa Process

Our 20-step car spa is a meticulous process that takes 4-6 hours. On the exterior, we perform a high-pressure rinse, active foam wash, and wheel detailing. We use a clay bar treatment to remove embedded iron particles and follow it with machine polishing and a ceramic wax application. For the interior, we perform deep extraction vacuuming, clean the carpets and headliner, and condition the leather or fabric seats. We clean the dashboard, door jambs, and glass panels. We sanitize the AC vents using steam and apply an ozone treatment to permanently eliminate odors. Finally, we wipe down the engine bay and apply a protective dressing for a complete restoration.
