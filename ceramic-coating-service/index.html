Why Preparation Decides the Result

A ceramic coating is only as good as the surface beneath it. If applied over bonded grime, swirl marks or water spots, it simply seals those flaws in. That's why every Carmaa coating starts with a thorough wash, clay decontamination and paint correction — so the ceramic bonds to clean, smooth paint and delivers its full gloss and durability.

Because the coating cures permanently, application technique matters. Our certified detailers work in a controlled way at your doorstep for an even, long-lasting finish.

Frequently Asked Questions

The longer-duration plans use more layers and higher-grade ceramic with more thorough paint prep, giving stronger gloss, water beading and durability. 1-Year is ₹7499, 3-Year is ₹14999, and 5-Year is ₹24999.

With proper care, the coating lasts for the rated duration (1, 3 or 5 years). Regular gentle washing and avoiding harsh chemicals helps it last its full life.

Ceramic coating adds a hard protective layer that resists swirl marks and minor marring and makes washing safer, but it is not scratch-proof. For impact and stone-chip protection, paint protection film (PPF) is the stronger option.

A full ceramic coating, including wash, decontamination, correction and curing, typically takes several hours up to a day depending on the plan and your car's condition.

We recommend waiting about 7 days before the first wash to let the coating fully cure, then washing gently with pH-neutral shampoo. Your detailer will share full aftercare guidance.
