    Post Car Care Recommendations After Ceramic Coating

    Instruction Description
    1 Do Not Use a Dirty Cloth Always use a clean, damp, high-quality microfiber towel for wiping. A dirty cloth can scratch the fresh coating.
    2 pH-Neutral Wash Only When washing after the first week, use a pH-neutral car shampoo or ceramic-safe soap to maintain the coating’s hydrophobic properties.
    3 No Automatic Brush Car Washes Brushes can create micro-scratches and reduce coating life. Opt for hand washing with microfiber mitts.
    4 Use the Two-Bucket Method One bucket for soapy water, one for rinsing the mitt/towel. This avoids transferring dirt back onto the paint.
    5 Boost the Coating Every 2–3 Months Use a ceramic booster spray to refresh shine and hydrophobicity.
    6 Wash Only in Shade Direct sunlight can cause water spots and soap streaking on the coating.
    7 Dry with a Clean Microfiber Never let the car air dry; water spots can etch into the coating.
    8 No Harsh Chemicals Avoid acidic or highly alkaline cleaners; they can weaken the coating.
    9 Topup Coat for Maximum Protection For up to 12 months of durability, apply next coat after every 3 month.
