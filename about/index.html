About Carmaa

The Future of Doorstep Car Cleaning Services

What is Carmaa’s mission in the Indian car care industry?

At Carmaa Car Care, established in January 2024, our mission is to revolutionize vehicle maintenance by offering doorstep, eco-friendly, and waterless solutions. Backed by our successful cleaning of 44,000+ cars, we aim to make high-quality car care accessible while reducing environmental impact through innovation and sustainable practices in every city we serve, including Noida and Dehradun.

How does Carmaa use Technology Business Incubation (TBI) for innovation?

Carmaa is proud to be incubated at the Technology Business Incubator (TBI), Graphic Era (Deemed to be University). This partnership grants us access to state-of-the-art facilities and expert mentorship. Being a DPIIT-recognized startup, we leverage this ecosystem to scale our lithium-ion powered equipment and scratch-free chemical polymers, ensuring the most advanced car care for our 44k+ customers.

Why is Carmaa considered a sustainable car washing service?

We have saved over 3.5 million liters of water by replacing traditional high-pressure hoses with our proprietary dry-cleaning technology. Every car wash at Carmaa saves 200 liters of water, aligning with 2026 global sustainability standards. Our commitment to 100% customer satisfaction is reflected in our track record of serving 150+ residential societies with zero-waste professional detailing.

Our Mission, Vision, Values

Our Values

We are guided by values of professionalism, quality, and sustainability. We believe in providing services that not only meet but exceed customer expectations, ensuring their vehicles look their best while protecting the environment.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the most trusted and innovative car care provider in India, known for our exceptional service and environmental stewardship. We strive to set new standards in the car detailing industry by combining cutting-edge techniques with a focus on customer convenience and satisfaction.

Our Mission

At Carmaa, our mission is to revolutionize the car cleaning industry by offering eco-friendly, convenient, and high-quality car care services directly to your doorstep. We aim to make car maintenance hassle-free and accessible, ensuring that every customer receives personalized care for their vehicle. By embracing waterless technology and sustainable practices, we are committed to reducing water waste while delivering spotless results.
