Everything You Need to Know About Doorstep Car Care

Expert insights into our eco-friendly, high-performance detailing world.

Is a waterless car wash really safe for my car's paint?

It definitely is! At Carmaa, we use specialized lubricants that wrap around dirt particles and lift them away without scrubbing. It’s actually safer than traditional washing because it avoids those tiny "swirl mark" scratches.

How much time and water can I really save with Carmaa?

A lot! While a normal wash wastes 200+ liters, we use less than 2 liters. Plus, you save hours of driving and waiting at a garage since we come directly to your parking spot.

Why should I pick doorstep detailing instead of a local car wash?

It's all about convenience and quality. You get professional-grade detailing while you relax at home. No traffic, no queues, and you can see exactly what products we’re using on your car.

Do I need to give your team any water or a power outlet?

Not at all! Our service vans are like mobile workshops—they carry their own water and electricity. We just need your car keys and enough space to walk around the vehicle.

Can you clean my car in a narrow basement or a busy street?

Yes, we can. Because our method is compact and doesn't involve splashing water everywhere, we’re able to work in tight spots where traditional washing just isn't possible.

What if my car is really muddy? Does waterless still work?

Our tech is great for everyday city dirt, dust, and rain spots. If your car is caked in heavy off-road mud, we might suggest a quick rinse first, but for 95% of cars, our method works perfectly.

How do I book a slot and how do I pay?

Just download the "Carmaa Car Care" app! You can pick your time, track the technician, and pay securely using UPI, cards, or net banking—all in a few taps.

Are your cleaning chemicals safe for kids and pets?

Safety is our priority. We use eco-friendly, non-toxic, and pH-neutral products. They’re tough on dirt but completely safe for your car’s interior and your family.

What if I’m not home when the technician arrives?

No worries! As long as we have access to your car, you don't need to be there. We’ll send you photos and updates through the app so you know the job's done right.

Do you have any monthly plans for regular cleaning?

We do! If you like keeping your car clean all the time, our subscription packages in the app are a great way to save money while ensuring your car always looks its best.

What exactly happens during a 'Car Premium Spa'?

Think of it as a total refresh. We deep-clean the exterior, vacuum every inch of the interior, polish the dashboard, and dress the tires. It’s the ultimate treat for your car.

What happens if I need to cancel my booking last minute?

We get it, plans change! You can reschedule or cancel through the app up to 2 hours before your slot. It’s completely hassle-free and easy to manage on the go.

How much does a doorstep car wash cost in Dehradun?

Pricing in Dehradun is very competitive! It depends on your car size and the package you choose. You can see exact, transparent pricing for your specific model directly in the Carmaa app before you book.

Does Carmaa work in apartment parking lots?

Yes, all the time! Our waterless method is ideal for apartments in Noida or Gurgaon because we don't leave puddles or mess. We clean your car right in its assigned spot without bothering your neighbors.

Is a doorstep car wash actually worth the money?

Definitely. When you factor in the fuel you save navigating traffic in Faridabad or Ghaziabad, the 2-3 hours of your time regained, and the professional-grade products we use, it’s often more cost-effective than traditional garage washes.

How often should I really get my car washed?

For most city cars, a professional clean once every two weeks keeps the paint protected. If you drive daily in dusty areas, a weekly "Quick Wash" is the best way to maintain that showroom shine.

Ceramic coating vs. waxing—which one is better?

Waxing is great for a short-term glow, but Ceramic Coating is the ultimate long-term protection. It creates a hard, hydrophobic shield that lasts for years, making your car much easier to clean.

What’s the difference between interior detailing and dry cleaning?

Dry cleaning focuses on removing stains from fabrics and upholstery. Interior detailing is a complete deep-clean—vacuuming every crack, polishing the dash, sanitizing surfaces, and glass cleaning.
