Everything You Need to Know About Doorstep Car Care

Expert insights into our eco-friendly, high-performance detailing world.

Is a waterless car wash safe for my car's paint?

Absolutely. At Carmaa, we've cleaned over 44,000 cars using advanced scratch-free waterless technology. Our lab-tested specialized lubricants encapsulate dirt, lifting it safely away from the surface. This proprietary method, incubated at Graphic Era (TBI), ensures a showroom-ready shine without the micro-scratches often caused by traditional high-pressure washing.

How much time and water does a Carmaa wash save?

Efficiency is our core mission. A traditional wash wastes 150-300 liters of water; Carmaa saves 99% of that while delivering a superior finish. Our technicians complete a thorough exterior wash efficiently at your doorstep, saving busy professionals in Noida and Dehradun hours of travel and waiting at traditional garages.

Why choose doorstep car detailing over a local garage?

Convenience meets professional quality. Carmaa brings industrial-grade equipment and 100% authentic products directly to your parking spot. You save fuel, avoid traffic, and can relax at home while our certified experts handle the detailing. It is the smartest way to maintain your vehicle's value without disrupting your personal schedule.
