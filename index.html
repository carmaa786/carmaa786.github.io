Everything You Need to Know About Doorstep Car Care

Expert insights into our eco-friendly, high-performance detailing world.

Is a waterless car wash really safe for my car's paint?

Yes, waterless car wash is completely safe for your car's paint. At Carmaa, we use specialized lubricants that wrap around dirt particles and lift them away without scrubbing — actually safer than traditional washing because it avoids the tiny swirl mark scratches caused by abrasive contact.

How much time and water can I really save with Carmaa?

You save over 200 liters of water and 2–3 hours per wash. While a normal wash wastes 200+ liters, we use less than 2 liters. Plus, you skip the drive and wait entirely since we come directly to your parking spot.

Why should I pick doorstep detailing instead of a local car wash?

Doorstep detailing gives you professional results without leaving home. You get certified technicians, premium products, and full visibility of the work being done — no traffic, no queues, no compromised quality from rushed local washers.

Do I need to give your team any water or a power outlet?

No — Carmaa's team is 100% self-sufficient. Our service vans carry their own water and power supply. We just need your car keys and enough space to walk around the vehicle.

Can you clean my car in a narrow basement or a busy street?

Yes, we regularly clean cars in tight basement parkings and narrow lanes. Because our waterless method involves no buckets, hoses, or drainage, we can work in spaces where traditional washing is simply not possible.

What if my car is really muddy? Does waterless still work?

For 95% of cars, our waterless technology handles heavy dirt and mud effectively. Our polymer cleaners encapsulate and lift particles safely. Only for extreme off-road mud do we suggest a light pre-rinse — everyday city grime and dust are fully handled.

Are your cleaning chemicals safe for kids and pets?

Yes — all Carmaa products are non-toxic, pH-neutral, and eco-certified. They are tough on dirt but completely safe for children, pets, and all car interior surfaces including leather and fabric.

What if I’m not home when the technician arrives?

You don't need to be home — we just need access to your car. Our technician will complete the service and send you before/after photos and updates through the app so you can verify the work remotely.

What exactly happens during a 'Car Premium Spa'?

Car Premium Spa is a full restoration service covering every surface inside and outside your car. It includes deep extraction vacuuming, dashboard conditioning, exterior polishing, hydrophobic paint treatment, and tire dressing.

Does Carmaa work in apartment parking lots?

Yes — apartment basement parking is our most common service location. Our waterless method leaves zero puddles or mess, making it ideal for societies in Noida or Gurgaon where traditional washing is restricted.

Is a doorstep car wash actually worth the money?

Yes — when you account for fuel saved, time regained, and professional products used, doorstep wash is often cheaper than a local garage visit. You save a 5–10km commute, 2–3 hours of waiting, and get certified technicians using better products than most local washers in Faridabad or Ghaziabad.

How often should I really get my car washed?

For city cars, a professional exterior wash every 2 weeks keeps paint protected. If you drive daily in dusty areas, a weekly Quick Wash is ideal. Interior deep cleaning is recommended every 30–45 days.

Ceramic coating vs. waxing—which one is better?

Ceramic coating is superior for long-term protection; waxing is better for a quick short-term shine. Ceramic creates a hard hydrophobic shield lasting 2–5 years. Wax lasts 1–3 months. For daily-driven cars in North India's harsh climate, ceramic coating offers significantly better value.

What’s the difference between interior detailing and dry cleaning?

Interior detailing is a complete deep-clean; dry cleaning targets fabric stains specifically. Detailing covers vacuuming every crevice, polishing the dashboard, sanitizing all surfaces, and cleaning glass. Dry cleaning focuses on removing stains and odors from upholstery and carpets only.
